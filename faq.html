What is Amaca Sports ?

It is a very easy to use application which allows you to organise any outdoor sport session with no headache. It facilitates you scheduling and logistics of your sport activities while getting the best renting deals.

How does it work ?

Select Any Sport you want
You can browse our selection of partners or look for a specific sport.
Book your event
Select when and where you want to go. Click. It's done.
Enjoy !
Have a great session, and don't forget to invite your friends !

I am a professional sport renter. How is Amacasport.com interesting for me?

As a professionnal, you will be able to post rental offers. It is very quick and simple to offer to rent equipment from your shop. Using Amaca, you will be able to:
  • Attract more client
  • Manage your stocks and your schedule
  • be known as a professional renter in your region and in France.
  • get visibility on the net, with a SEO facilitated by our website

I am a professional sport Renter. How can I use your platform ?

All you have to do is to create an account and to tick the 'private' checkbox. Then just use the button 'Post an offer' in order to propose your products to be rented.

I am a private individual and I would like to rent my equipment. Is it really a good idea ? Is it safe?

Yes. Working with Amaca will allow you to:
  • reimburse your equipment
  • make additionnal money
  • have interaction with people that probably share the same passion as you.
Also, Amaca will provide a deposit system that insure you against any trouble with your equipment.
